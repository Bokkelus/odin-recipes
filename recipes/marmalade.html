Carrot Marmalade

A lovely and tangy marmalade. Done before you know it

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of tap water
  • 2 tablespoons grated orange zest plus 1/2 cup fresh juice
  • 4 cups grated carrots
  • 4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tablespoons kosher salt

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients

  2. Comgine water,orangezes and juice,lemon zest and juiste, and carrots in a large saucepan.

    Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally. recude heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, until carrots are tender-crisp, about 15 minutes

  3. stir in sugar, cardamom, and salt until sugar is dissolved, about 30 seconds. simmer over medium-low, stirring occasionally, until thickened and a candy thermometer registers 220 degrees f, 40 to 45 minutes.
  4. skim and discard any foam off top. pour carrot mixture into 16-ounce jar
  5. cover with lid and refrigerate until fully cooled, atleast 4 hours or up to 3 weeks.
